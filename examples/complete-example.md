# Product Launch 2024

## Overview

This plan tracks all activities for our Q1 2024 product launch. The goal is to
acquire 1000 new users in the first month.

## Strategy

Our go-to-market strategy focuses on:

1. **Content marketing** - Blog posts, guides, and tutorials
2. **Social media** - Twitter, LinkedIn, and IndieHackers
3. **Product Hunt** - Coordinated launch with community support
4. **Paid ads** - Google Ads and LinkedIn Ads (limited budget)

## Key Metrics

- Website visitors: 10,000
- Signups: 1,000 (10% conversion)
- Paid conversions: 100 (10% of signups)
